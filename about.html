About Us

Founded by former Division I athletes, Gentle Giant Training exists to help football players build confidence, sharpen their game, and prepare for meaningful opportunities beyond high school.

M

Our Mission

Develop complete athletes who are physically prepared, mentally disciplined, and ready to represent themselves well on the field, in the classroom, and in the recruiting process.

V

Our Vision

Create a trusted football development program that equips hundreds of athletes with elite preparation, scholarship opportunities, and strong character.

Our Journey

2015

Founded

Gentle Giant Training launched with a mission to mentor athletes through elite instruction and consistent accountability.

2018

First 100 Athletes

The program expanded quickly as more athletes and families looked for technical coaching with a personal approach.

2021

Facility Expansion

Training space grew to support larger group sessions, off-season speed work, and position-specific development.

2024

$10M in Scholarships

Athletes from the program earned major scholarship opportunities through strong preparation and consistent guidance.
